Testing with Pytest:

Testing with pytest is a method of automating the testing and therefore debugging of programs. Manually entering in variable cases to test is extremely tedious and time consuming, and is far more prone to human error. Additionally, rerunning tests manually after debugging is infuriating. Pytest allows for multiple tests to be written faster and ran automatically, with easy to understand feedback. I utilized pytest in the calculator demo to ensure the output was correct and the constructors functioned.


Simple Pages:

I less used simple page tests as much as I battled with it. I didn't fully understand the issue with the pytest at first, but once I recognized there was assertions preventing pages from being renamed I altered tests to accept the more correct input
